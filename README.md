# 🏠 Airbnb Market Insights Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

The Airbnb Market Insights Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze Airbnb listings, pricing trends, host activity, property types, and guest ratings across multiple cities.

This dashboard transforms raw Airbnb data into meaningful business insights through data visualization, KPI tracking, and trend analysis, helping stakeholders better understand market performance and customer behavior.

## 📈 Key Insights

### Market Growth
- Airbnb experienced rapid growth between 2011 and 2016.
- Listing activity reached its peak around 2015–2016.
- Growth slowed after the peak period.
- The COVID-19 pandemic negatively impacted listing activity.

### Host Analysis
- Paris recorded the highest number of listings.
- New York remained one of the strongest Airbnb markets.
- Host distribution varied significantly across cities.

### Pricing Trends
- Hotel Rooms showed the highest average pricing.
- Entire Places remained the most popular accommodation type.
- Shared Rooms offered the most budget-friendly option.

### Customer Satisfaction
- Mexico City achieved the highest average guest rating.
- Most cities maintained consistently strong customer ratings.
- Customer satisfaction remained high across different property categories.
